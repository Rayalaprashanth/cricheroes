World Cricket Championship (WCC) is a series of 3D cricket mobile games developed by Nextwave Multimedia. WCC is the first game of the WCC franchise which was launched and released in 2011. The updated versions WCC2, WCC Rivals and WCC3 were also released under the WCC franchise. Within the month of March in 2020, WCC was ranked as the no. 1 cricket mobile game franchise in the world with 110 million downloads worldwide and with approximately 11 million monthly active users.[1][2] It has reported an average of over 2.1 million daily active users and is one of the most downloaded cricket game franchises in the world
